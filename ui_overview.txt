1. Enable Remote Monitoring: Allow neurologists to track Parkinson's patients' symptoms (tremor, bradykinesia, rigidity) in real-
time through a secure web platform.
2. *AI-Driven Emergency Alerts*: Implement an AI engine that detects critical patterns (e.g., sustained tremor, falls) and instantly 
alerts doctors via SMS and in-app notifications.
3. *Centralize Medical Data*: Store all patient reports (MRI, DaTscan, blood tests) in a HIPAA-compliant Google Drive vault linked 
to each patient's unique ID.
4. *Secure Doctor-Controlled Therapy*: Provide neurologists with the ability to trigger approved therapy (e.g., shock waves via 
implanted device) with mandatory 2-factor authentication and immutable audit logs.
5. *Improve Clinical Outcomes*: Reduce emergency response time, decrease hospital readmissions, and provide objective data to
doctors for better treatment decisions – ultimately improving Parkinson's patient quality of life.

--- Page 10 ---
• The NeuroTrack platform successfully demonstrated the feasibility of remote 
monitoring for Parkinson's disease patients, enabling timely interventions and 
personalized care. By integrating wearable sensors, AI-driven alerts, and a secure 
web interface, we achieved:
• - Near real-time symptom tracking with >95% accuracy
• - Detection of 78 clinically significant events
• - Positive feedback from 9/10 neurologists
• - HIPAA-compliant data handling
• This proof-of-concept paves the way for larger clinical trials and potential regulatory 
approval. Future work includes expanding to more patients, adding predictive 
analytics, and integrating with electronic health records.
